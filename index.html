About Me

Hello! My Name is David Huang, and I'm an aspiring software developer. I majored in Philosophy while studying at Boston University and somehow found my way to programming through a philosophy class in logic. My professor exclaimed, "If you like logic, then you should take a Computer Science class! The symbolic logic we use in this class is the same type of boolean logic used in computer science!" With my professor's encouragement, I took a CS course and haven't looked back, and finished my degree at BU with a minor in Computer Science. I'm looking to continue building my toolkit of programming skills and showcase some of the things I've been working on here!
